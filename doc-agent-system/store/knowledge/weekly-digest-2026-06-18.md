Weekly Digest gives you a Monday morning summary of notifications that are useful to review but may not need immediate interruption. It is delivered every Monday at 8AM in your local timezone. The digest is designed for catch-up: it gathers missed Normal notifications, Low-priority highlights, and grouped updates from the previous week so you can understand what happened without opening every individual notification.

The digest never delays Critical notifications. Security alerts, billing failures, outage notices, and required action messages still appear when they are sent, including during Quiet Hours if the product requires immediate visibility. Weekly Digest focuses on Normal notifications that were held during Quiet Hours and Low notifications that are marked digest-eligible. This makes the digest helpful without turning it into a substitute for urgent delivery.

Inside the digest, updates are grouped by source and feature area. A workflow product might show assigned tasks together, while product education updates appear in a separate section. Each group includes concise titles and enough preview text to help you decide whether to open the full inbox. When you need more detail, use the link in the digest to jump directly to Smart Notification Center.

You can disable Weekly Digest from notification settings. If you keep it enabled, expect delivery at 8AM every Monday according to your current local timezone. If you travel, the scheduled delivery follows the timezone recognized by the account or device settings used by the app. The best use of the digest is as a weekly review habit: scan it at the start of the week, open anything that needs follow-up, and archive items you no longer need in the unified inbox.
