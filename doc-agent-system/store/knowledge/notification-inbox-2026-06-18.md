The Notification Inbox is the central place to review every notification type in Smart Notification Center v2.0. Instead of checking separate areas for reminders, account messages, workflow updates, product tips, and announcements, you can open one inbox and see a chronological list of active notification records from the last 90 days. Each notification includes its source, title, preview text, timestamp, unread state, and priority badge.

Priority badges help you understand what needs attention first. Critical notifications are reserved for security, billing failure, outage, and required action alerts. Normal notifications usually include assigned tasks, workflow changes, mentions, and reminders. Low notifications include tips, product education, marketing updates, and optional summaries. Critical notifications remain visually distinct so they are easy to spot even when the inbox contains many items.

You can filter the inbox by Critical, Normal, or Low priority. Filtering does not delete or hide messages permanently; it simply narrows the list so you can focus. For example, you might filter to Critical when checking urgent items, then switch to Normal when reviewing assigned work. Low-priority messages are useful for learning about product changes but generally do not require immediate action.

After reading a notification, you can archive it from the list or from the detail view. Archiving removes the item from the active inbox while preserving the record for audit and history experiences supported by the product. If a notification includes a next step, such as reviewing a workflow change or opening billing settings, the action appears in the detail view so you can move directly from awareness to completion.
